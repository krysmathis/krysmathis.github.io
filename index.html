I am a member of cohort 22 @ Nashville Software School

I chose to become a software developer because I love to solve problems that help people and businesses do more with less. Looking back, my most rewarding experiences have been projects where I was able help people leverage technology and eliminate wasted effort. I've included a link to my resume, but here are a few highlights of where I've been.

My most recent experience was as the Director of Demand Planning at Dollar General. The Demand Planning team is obsessed with the future. We spent a majority of our time forecasting and thinking about the future. We were also responsible for building an maintaining many of the tools used by the supply chain team.

It was during my time with the company that I started developing 'software' with whatever technology I had on hand. I was often the first stop when people encountered system or technical issues or if they had a great idea but didn't know how to implement it. Instead of continuing to give unqualified advice, I decided to leverage NSS to help me make the move to professional software developer.
